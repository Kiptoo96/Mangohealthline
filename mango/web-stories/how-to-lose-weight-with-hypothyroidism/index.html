How to Lose Weight with Hypothyroidism

Learn more!

Having hypothyroidism (underactive thyroid) can make it hard to lose weight - but not impossible!

These science-backed tips will help you finally lose weight, and improve your thyroid health!

Learn more!

Check Your TSH Levels

1

If you're being treated for hypothyroidism, and still not losing weight, ask your doctor to check your exact TSH (thyroid-stimulating hormone) levels.

You may need your doctor's help to lower them further to see results.

Learn more

Add in T3

2

Normal hypothyroidism treatment uses synthetic T4 hormone, but some studies have shown that treatment with both T3 and T4 hormone produces better weight loss results.

Learn more

Boost Your Metabolism

3

Hypothyroidism can cause your metabolism to slow.

Adding in daily exercise is a great way to rev up your body's ability to burn calories.

Learn more

Eat for Health

4

Eating healthy, high-fiber, low-sugar foods in small, frequent meals throughout the day can help regulate your hormone levels naturally. 

Learn more

Be Your Own Advocate

5

If you’re working with your doctor but not seeing the results that you expect from your treatment, speak up and change things up!
